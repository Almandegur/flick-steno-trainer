Parameters · threshold and calibration

Finger closure · range-of-motion check

Each bar = how far that finger traveled through its own range (0% open to 100% full fist). The white marker is its close threshold. A finger only registers when its bar passes the marker, so a neighbor half-curling (anatomical coupling) stays below and is ignored.
